Lifestyle Yoga

Lifestyle Yoga is a project to bring short and accessible yoga classes to working professionals. The classes will be a half hour in length and will take place before 9AM and after 5PM. All classes will also be free of charge as the teachers will volunteer their time. The project is currently on hold.
